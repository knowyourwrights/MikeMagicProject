Fire Charm

Ingredients:

  • Dragon dust 500 g
  • Ember petals 30 mg
  • Smoke crystal 7 mg

HOW TO USE

The Fire Charm is a powerful and ancient spell, capable of conjuring bright flames without the risk of uncontrolled fires. The caster can illuminate dark spaces, defend themselves from hostile creatures, or create magical fire displays. The flames have an intensity proportional to the caster's concentration and can be shaped into particular shapes and movements
