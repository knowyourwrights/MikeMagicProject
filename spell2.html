The Healing Mist is an ancient and gentle spell, capable of bringing immediate relief to those who are wounded. When cast, a soft, luminous mist envelops the surrounding area, penetrating the skin and soul. This mist regenerates tissue and restores energetic balance, alleviating pain and fatigue. The spell is also perfect for healing animals or plants
