Shadow Whisper

Ingredients:

  • Nightshade root 1pc
  • Black candle soot 80 g
  • Silent fog crystal 7 mg

HOW TO USE

The Shadow Whisper is an ancient spell that harnesses the energy of shadows to reveal what is hidden from ordinary eyes. The caster can hear secrets whispered from dark places and sense invisible presences. The spell is never aggressive; it acts discreetly, enveloping the caster in a light veil of shadow
